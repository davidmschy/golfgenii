The Champion We Built Together

In 2012, Bryson DeChambeau walked into Mike Schy's academy as a promising junior golfer. Three years later, he became the first player to win both the NCAA Individual Championship and the U.S. Amateur in the same year since 1960.

This wasn't luck. This was the result of a revolutionary coaching system that combined mental training, technical precision, and performance psychology into a framework that produces champions.

Today, Bryson is a two-time major champion with 13 PGA Tour victories. But the system that built him isn't just for tour professionals anymore.
